GOLD INVESTMENT 101

Discover the Best Ways to Invest in Gold

by Meyisanen Longkumer

Investing in gold is a popular choice for those looking to diversify their portfolios and hedge against economic uncertainties. As we all know that gold has been valued for its rarity and beauty for thousands of years and continues to be a reliable store of value.

Here, we spoke with Srikanth V, an expert in the precious metals market and the Founder and Director of Sristar Gold and Sristar Sona Chandi Limited, to explore various gold investment options, including physical gold, ETFs, gold mining stocks, and more.

1. Physical Gold

Forms of Physical Gold:

Gold Bullion

Available in bars and coins, gold bullion is a tangible asset that investors can hold. The value is determined by the gold content and current market prices.

Jewelry

While commonly bought for adornment, gold jewelry can also be considered an investment. However, it often carries higher premiums due to craftsmanship and design.

Gold Coins

Coins such as the American Gold Eagle, Canadian Gold Maple Leaf, and South African Krugerrand are popular among investors for their purity and recognized value.

1. Physical Gold

Advantages üëç

Tangible Asset: Physical gold is a real, tangible asset that can be securely stored.

No Counterparty Risk and Inflation Hedge: Physical gold does not rely on a third party‚Äôs ability to pay and retains its value, making it a good hedge against inflation.

Disadvantages üëé

Storage and Insurance: Safekeeping physical gold requires storage and insurance, increasing cost.

Liquidity Issues: Selling physical gold can be less straightforward and involve fees.

2. Gold Exchange-Traded Funds (ETFs)

What are Gold ETFs? Gold ETFs are investment funds traded on stock exchanges. They track the price of gold and are backed by physical holdings.

Advantages üëç

High Liquidity and No Storage Issues: Gold ETFs can be easily bought and sold, no need for storage.

Cost-Effective: Lower transaction costs compared to buying physical gold.

Disadvantages üëé

Management Fees: Annual fees may erode returns over time.

Counterparty Risk: Though minimal, there is risk with financial institutions managing ETFs.

Popular Gold ETFs:

  • SPDR Gold Shares
  • iShares Gold Trust (IAU)
  • Aberdeen Standard Physical
  • Gold Shares ETF

3. Gold Mining Stocks

What are Gold Mining Stocks? Shares of companies engaged in gold extraction and production. Investing gives exposure to gold prices and company performance.

Popular Gold Mining Companies:

  • Barrick Gold Corporation
  • Newmont Corporation
  • AngloGold Ashanti

4. Gold Mutual Funds

What are Gold Mutual Funds?

These funds invest in a diversified portfolio of gold-related assets, including physical gold, gold mining stocks, and gold ETFs.

Advantages üëç

Potential for High Returns: Gold mining stocks can offer significant returns if the company performs well and gold prices rise.

Dividends: Some gold mining companies pay dividends to their shareholders.

Disadvantages üëé

Management Fees: These funds charge management fees, which can reduce overall returns.

Less Control: Investors have less control over individual asset selection compared to direct investment in stocks or physical gold.

5. Gold Futures and Options

What are Gold Futures and Options?

Gold Futures

Contracts to buy or sell a specific amount of gold at a predetermined price on a set date in the future.

Gold Options

Contracts that give the holder the right, but not the obligation, to buy or sell gold at a specific price before a certain date.

6. Digital Gold

What is Digital Gold?

Digital gold platforms allow investors to buy and hold gold in a digital form. The gold is stored in secure vaults, and investors can buy or sell it online.

Advantages
  • Convenience: Easy to buy, sell, and hold gold digitally.
  • Low Transaction Costs: Lower costs compared to physical gold transactions.
Disadvantages
  • Storage Fees: Some platforms may charge storage fees.
  • Regulatory Risks: Digital gold platforms may face regulatory changes.

Gold investment options provide various ways to gain exposure to the precious metal, each with its own advantages and risks. Diversifying across physical gold, ETFs, digital gold, and mining companies can balance benefits and risks effectively.
