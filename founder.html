About the Founders

SriStar Gold Company was founded in 2019 by Mr. Srikanth V. Nalige and Mr. Manjappa Bidaragaddi with a vision to create a trusted, thriving gold trading platform across India. Their mission is deeply rooted in empowering Indian buying power through locally made products and services, while also contributing to the nation's economic growth.

– Srikanth V. Nalige & Manjappa Bidaragaddi

SriStar Gold Company

Leveraging their expertise in finance and gold retail, the founders developed a business model that benefits everyone. Operations in gold buying and selling generate substantial revenue, boost India’s economy, and prioritize transparent, ethical transactions that provide financial security to individuals.

Going the Extra Mile

SriStar Gold Company’s growing franchise network across India provides lucrative career opportunities and promising annual returns. The expansion into Karnataka and Tamil Nadu has opened doors to diverse employment options, promoting both individual growth and community development.
