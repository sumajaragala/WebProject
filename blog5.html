KNOW YOUR GOLD

By Dhruv Taneja

In an industry as timeless and dynamic as gold trading, Srikanth V, the Founder and Director of SriStar Gold and SriStar Sona Chandi Limited, has etched a unique mark. His innovative approach to gold buying and selling across Karnataka and Tamil Nadu has revolutionized the industry.

The story of SriStar Gold is one of humble beginnings and ambitious vision. Srikanth reflects, "We initially started from scratch, but over the years we have now successfully established twelve (12) centers across Karnataka and two (2) in Tamil Nadu. Our core business is not just buying gold but also releasing pledged gold. This unique model offers relief to those struggling with the burden of interest on their pledged gold."

For Srikanth, gold is not just a metal; it's a symbol of enduring value. He says, "Gold is a super element, unique in its characteristics. Its non-tarnishable nature and high resale value set it apart from other metals."

He further explains gold purity: "24-carat gold, being the purest form, is too malleable for jewelry. Hence, we often mix it with metals like zinc, silver, and copper to create 22-carat gold, suitable for ornamentation."

Highlight: "Investing in gold serves two main purposes. Firstly, as a secure investment, it offers unparalleled liquidity. Secondly, in times of emergency, like medical or educational needs, gold stands as a reliable resource."

In an industry rife with concerns about authenticity and scams, Srikanth discusses the measures ensuring transparency. The introduction of the Hallmark Unique Identification (HUID) and BIS hallmarking by the Indian government has enhanced industry transparency. Customers can now use tools like the BIS Care app to verify authenticity of their gold.

Srikanth emphasizes the importance of the HUID: “Each gold ornament must have a 76-digit alphanumeric HUID code, key to confirming authenticity. Through BIS Care, customers can cross-verify this information.”

Explaining why customers should choose SriStar Gold, Srikanth says, “Customer satisfaction is our branding.” They employ XRF technology to assess purity non-invasively, ensuring transparency. Additionally, they offer gold purchases without extra charges, making the process customer-friendly.

SriStar Gold sets new standards in transparency, trading, and customer satisfaction — making gold trading more accessible to the common person.
